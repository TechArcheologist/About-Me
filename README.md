🏛️ Featured Upcoming Projects
🏛️ Project 1 — Ancient Stone Inscription OCR
Computer Vision · Deep Learning
A thousand years ago, dynasties like the Cholas carved extensive records into temple stone walls and palm leaves. Most of it sits unread today — not lost, just illegible to anyone without decades of scholarly training in scripts like Vatteluttu, Grantha, and ancient Brahmi.
The Goal: A model that takes a single mobile-phone photo of an eroded stone inscription and translates it into modern, readable text.
How it's built:
Sourcing open historical script image datasets from archaeology department archives
OpenCV-based preprocessing — denoising, crack-sharpening, and character isolation on worn stone surfaces
A CNN or transformer-based OCR architecture (TrOCR-style) trained to classify and reconstruct ancient characters
Why it matters:
🏢 Corporate value: demonstrates elite-level computer vision, custom image-preprocessing pipelines, and from-scratch dataset engineering — the kind of applied CV work most portfolios only simulate.
🧭 Explorer value: walk up to an untouched ruin, scan the wall, and read its history on the spot.

🛰️ Project 2 — Satellite Anomaly Detector for Lost Ruins
Geospatial Data Science · Remote Sensing
Temples, roads, and burial grounds buried for a millennium leave faint geometric fingerprints in soil and vegetation — invisible on the ground, but detectable from orbit.
The Goal: An AI pipeline that scans satellite imagery and flags coordinates where a hidden structure is likely buried beneath the surface.
How it's built:
Google Earth Engine + open Sentinel-2 satellite imagery
Near-Infrared (NIR) band analysis to catch vegetation-health anomalies — buried walls restrict root growth, subtly altering the plant life directly above them
A U-Net segmentation model trained on open archaeological site datasets to detect architectural geometry from above
Why it matters:
🏢 Corporate value: proves fluency in geospatial analysis (GIS), remote sensing, and large-scale cloud-based geodata processing — high-demand, low-supply skills.
🧭 Explorer value: generate a shortlist of coordinates from your laptop, pack a bag, and go stand on the exact spot.
